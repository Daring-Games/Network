NETWORK

Network, in simple terms is an arrangement used to connect many objects. Network (in terms of Web), has a similar definition, it is used to connect many computers at a global level to create a huge network that can share information and data. For eg: the Internet.

Computer Network is the most useful e - resource in our lives. It provides us with many things: We can call our friends, we can visit the internet to get answers to our questions, we can our personal WiFi (PAN) to connect other devices and so on...
